About Haunted X Café - RAK

Experience a unique and thrilling café atmosphere at our Haunted X Café in RAK. This themed café combines delicious treats and beverages with a spooky, immersive environment that will delight horror fans and thrill-seekers alike.

With its later opening hours from 4 PM to 2 AM, Haunted X Café is the perfect spot for evening gatherings and late-night adventures. Our staff, themed decor, and occasional surprises create an unforgettable experience for all visitors.
